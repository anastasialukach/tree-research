The Loulou Test

Linear research gave Loulou Khazen Baz appearances: 0, episodes: [], linkedin: null, twitter: null — then wrote "huge fan of what you've built with Conversations with Loulou" in a sample outreach DM without knowing a single thing she'd built.

Tree research found: 95+ episodes, 6 named guests with episode titles, and discovered the MENA podcast circuit pattern — Loulou's show is the regional qualifying round before global shows like 20VC. That insight only exists because the tree went deep on one branch instead of wide across 118 entries.

The finding no linear search would surface

MENA is the biggest blind spot across ALL major LP content platforms. Capital Allocators has 700+ episodes with zero MENA sovereign wealth coverage. The same gap exists across Origins, How I Invest, Alt Goes Mainstream, and Swimming with Allocators. This insight only emerged because 5 parallel branches independently mapped different ecosystems — the gap is visible when you compare all 5 trees, not from any single one.
